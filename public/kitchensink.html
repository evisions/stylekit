Color Palette

Our colors were specifically chosen to create a light and inviting palette. Our goal is that these subtle colors allow us the ability to easily apply similar themes across multiple products to create more brand awareness. Going with shades of blue as our foundation will allows us to interchange them as primary and secondary colors to keep familiarity while giving different applications their own identity.

Buttons

We use different types of buttons for different types of interactions. We use our "Primary" button for the most important CTAs on pages, and use the basic "Grey" buttons for secondary CTAs. In addtion to color combinations, we insert iconography into buttons whenever we feel this enhances the interaction and makes it more clear to a user.
